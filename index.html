About

Mathew Sachin (born March 27, 1998) is an Indian student programmer. His interests are music and programming.

Developer - Musician - HUMAN?

Music

Mathew likes listening to English and Malayalam songs. Some of his favorites are Linkin Park, Enrique Iglesias, Owl City. He likes to sing though not proud of his voice. He learned to play the piano and the guitar a little.

Programming

He is particularly interested in C# .Net and has some experience in Java, C, C++ and Powershell. He started to program as a hobby in 8th standard beginning with Q-BASIC and soon realised the need for something more powerfull and tried Java while side by side exploring Powershell. Then he tried C and C++ and finally found his favorite C#. Also, he has tried web development (HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, AngularJS, Bootstrap, ASP.NET).

Education

Mathew passed 10th standard from Carmel Convent School (CCS), Faridabad in 2014. He passed 12th standard in Non-Medical stream from Modern Delhi Public School (MDPS), Faridabad in 2016. Currently, he is enrolled in a 5-year B-Tech + M-Tech Dual Degree (IT) program in Atal Bihari Vajpayee - Indian Institute of Information Technology (ABV-IIITM), Gwalior.
