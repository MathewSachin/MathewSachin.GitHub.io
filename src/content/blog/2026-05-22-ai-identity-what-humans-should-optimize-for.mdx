When capability becomes cheap, identity becomes strategic.

## Target query
If AI can do most tasks, what should people optimize for to stay useful and fulfilled?

## Promise
You will leave with a practical framework for skills, status, and meaning in an AI-heavy economy.

## Skills that age well
Judgment, taste, accountability, and domain-context synthesis outlast tool churn.

## Social status shifts
Output volume matters less when everyone has leverage; trust and reliability matter more.

## Meaning vs productivity
A fully optimized life is not always a meaningful one.

## Personal operating framework
Choose compounding domains, build reputation loops, and keep human commitments explicit.
