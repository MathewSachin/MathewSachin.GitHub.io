Just because you can run a script in DevTools does not mean you should publish every script.

## Target query
Where is the ethical line between harmless browser customization and abusive exploitation?

## Promise
You will get a framework to decide what is responsible to test, share, and refuse.

## Soft wall vs hard wall
Client-side presentation barriers are not equivalent to bypassing server-enforced access control.

## User safety and unintended harm
Avoid publishing techniques that directly increase account takeover, fraud, or privacy exposure risk.

## Legal and disclosure boundaries
Prefer educational analysis and responsible disclosure over exploit operationalization.

## What not to publish
If a method meaningfully enables abuse at scale, stop at concept level.
