#### How It Works

This tuner uses your browser's microphone and the
[Web Audio API](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Web_Audio_API)
to capture audio in real time and detect pitch through an autocorrelation algorithm.
No audio is ever sent to a server — everything runs locally in your browser.

##### Features

- **Chromatic mode:** Detects any pitch and displays the nearest note name, octave, and
  frequency in Hz with a ±50 cents deviation meter.
- **Acoustic guitar mode:** Highlights the nearest standard-tuning string (E2, A2, D3, G3,
  B3, E4) so you know which string you are tuning.
- **Visual meter:** A color-coded bar shows how flat (red) or sharp (red) the detected note
  is, turning green when you are within ±5 cents of the target.

##### Privacy

Your microphone audio is processed entirely in your browser. No audio data or pitch data
is transmitted anywhere.
