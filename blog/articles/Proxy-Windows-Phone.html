Using Windows Phone on Authenticated Proxy Wifi

No matter how much you like your Windows Phone, you hate it when you reach college where you are provided with authenticated proxy Wi-Fi. All your friends having Android phones enjoy the Wi-Fi while you are left behind.

Here's a way to use the Internet on your Windows Phone:

  1. Connect to the college Wi-Fi on your Windows computer.

  2. Download, Install and Start CCProxy on your Windows computer.
    CCProxy is easy-to-use and powerful proxy server.

  1. Open Options > Advanced > Cascading.

  1. Check Enable Cascading Proxy.

  2. Fill Proxy Address and Port.

  3. Select HTTP as Proxy Protocol.

  4. Check Need Authentication.

  5. Fill User Name and Password.

  6. Press OK.

  7. Now in Options, note the values of Local IP Address and HTTP Port.

  1. Make a Wi-Fi hotspot on your Windows computer. Windows 10 Anniversary Update and later include a built-in Wi-Fi hotspot feature. If you have an older OS, you could use a 3rd party software like Connectify.

  2. Connect your Windows Phone to your computer's Wi-Fi hotspot.

  3. On your Windows Phone, press and hold the name of your computer's Wi-Fi hotspot and click on Edit.

  4. Switch on Proxy.

  1. Enter values of Server and Port noted in Step 10.

  2. Press done.

Enjoy your college's Wi-Fi on your Windows Phone! Sadly, some apps may still not work, most notably, WhatsApp.
